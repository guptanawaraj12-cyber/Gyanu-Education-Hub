Frequently Asked Questions

📖 Are all notes really free?

Yes! 100% free. No hidden charges, no premium plans. Every note on Gyanu Notes is completely free to access and download.

📥 How can I download notes?

Click the "Download PDF" button on any chapter page. The PDF will open or download instantly. No login required!

🔐 Do I need to create an account?

No! You can access all notes without login. Creating a free account gives you extra features like saving favorites and tracking your study progress.

📚 Are notes updated with new syllabus?

Yes, we follow the latest NEB (Nepal) and CBSE (India) syllabus. Notes are regularly updated.

📝 Can I request notes for a specific topic?

Absolutely! Use this contact form to request any topic. We'll try our best to add it within a week.

❤️ How can I support Gyanu Notes?

Share with your friends, follow us on social media, and keep learning! Your support keeps us motivated.
