About Gyanu Notes

Your trusted partner in education free, accessible, and always growing.

Our Mission

To provide every Nepali student with free, high-quality educational resources regardless of their background or location. We believe that access to education should never be a barrier to success.

Our Vision

A Nepal where every student has access to the study materials they need to succeed in their exams and build a brighter future. We envision a community of learners, teachers, and supporters sharing knowledge freely.

Our Values

The principles that guide everything we do.

Quality

Curated, accurate, and exam-focused content. Every resource is carefully reviewed before being published.

Accessibility

Free for everyone, anytime, anywhere. No sign-ups, no logins, no hidden charges.

Community

Built by students, for students. We are a community of learners, teachers, and supporters.

Our Story

Gyanu Notes was born from a simple idea: education should be free.

As students ourselves, we know how expensive textbooks and study materials can be. We started this platform to share our own notes, question papers, and resources with fellow students across Nepal.

Today, Gyanu Notes has grown into a community of learners, teachers, and supporters who believe in the power of shared knowledge. We are constantly adding new content and we're just getting started!
