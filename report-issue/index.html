How to Report an Issue

We strive to provide the best experience on Gyanu Notes. If you encounter any issues, please let us know using one of the methods below.

Option 1: Email Us

Send an email to gyanunotes@gmail.com with details about the issue. Please include:

  • Page URL: Where did you find the issue?
  • Description: What happened? What did you expect to happen?
  • Screenshot: If possible, attach a screenshot.

Option 2: Use Our Contact Form

Visit our Contact Page and fill out the form. Select "Report Issue" in the subject field.

Common Issues We Can Fix

  • Broken Links: Links that don't work or go to the wrong page.
  • Missing Files: PDFs or files that fail to download.
  • Spelling or Grammar Errors: Mistakes in our content.
  • Outdated Information: Content that needs to be updated.
  • Mobile Issues: Problems viewing the site on your phone.

Our Commitment

  • Quick Response: We will acknowledge your report within 24 hours.
  • Action: We will investigate and resolve the issue as soon as possible.
  • Communication: We will inform you when the issue is resolved.

Thank You

Your reports help us improve Gyanu Notes for everyone. Thank you for helping us build a better learning platform for Nepali students!
