🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Information We Collect

Gyanu Education Hub does not collect any personal information unless you voluntarily provide it via our contact form or newsletter signup. The only information we collect is:

  • Name: Provided when you fill out the contact form.
  • Email Address: Provided when you fill out the contact form or subscribe to our newsletter.
  • Message Content: The message you send through our contact form.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and messages.
  • Send you educational updates (if you subscribe to our newsletter).
  • Improve our content and services based on your feedback.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

We do not use cookies for tracking. However, we use localStorage (a browser feature) to:

  • Remember your dark/light mode preference.

This data is stored only on your device and is not shared with us or any third party.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Web3Forms: To process contact form submissions. Your name, email, and message are sent to Web3Forms, which then forwards them to us via email. Please review Web3Forms' privacy policy for more details.
  • Google Drive: To host our PDF files. We do not share your personal data with Google Drive.

5. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any data you have submitted.
  • Unsubscribe from our newsletter at any time.
  • Contact us with any privacy concerns.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at gyanunotes@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
