About Gyanu Notes

Free study notes for every student in Nepal and beyond

Our Story

Gyanu Notes was founded with a simple mission: to provide high-quality, free study notes to every student in Nepal. We believe that quality education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of their financial situation.

What started as a small collection of personal notes has now grown into a comprehensive resource trusted by thousands of students across the country. Our team works tirelessly to create notes that are easy to understand, comprehensive, and completely free.

Today, Gyanu Notes helps students from Class 8 to Class 12 prepare for their exams with confidence. We cover all major subjects including Science, Mathematics, English, Nepali, Social Studies, Computer Science, and more.

Our Mission

To make quality education accessible to every student, anywhere, completely free of cost. We strive to empower students with the best study materials.

Our Vision

To become Nepal's most trusted free study platform, empowering thousands of students to achieve their dreams and excel in their academic journey.
